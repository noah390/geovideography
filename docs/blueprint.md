# **App Name**: FlagSphere

## Core Features:

- Flag Browse & Search: Users can browse a comprehensive collection of country flags or search for a specific country's flag by name.
- Country Information Display: Clicking on a flag displays essential information about the corresponding country, such as capital city and region.
- Interactive Flag Quiz: A generative AI tool that quizzes users on their knowledge of flags, asking them to identify a country from its flag image or vice-versa.
- Filter Flags by Continent: Allows users to filter the displayed flags by continent for easier navigation and discovery.
- Random Flag Showcase: Presents a random country flag and its name, perfect for casual exploration or learning.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant yet calming blue (#2663D9), reflecting knowledge and global connection.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#F0F1F5), providing a clean canvas for flag colors to stand out.
- Accent color: A bright, clear cyan (#31DBF5) to draw attention to interactive elements and highlight quiz answers.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for its modern, objective, and neutral appearance, ensuring high readability.
- Utilize simple, outline-style icons for navigation and controls to maintain a clean and contemporary feel.
- Implement a responsive grid layout for displaying flags, ensuring an organized and visually appealing experience across various device sizes. Use ample whitespace.
- Subtle hover effects on flags and smooth transitions when opening country details or moving to the next quiz question.
